FAQS

Quick answers to common questions – your FAQs resource awaits!

Booking
Can I cancel my booking at anytime?

You need to cancel at least 2 days in advance. If it rains before the lesson then the lesson will be cancelled. If rain interrupts a lesson, remaining time will be rescheduled, but the lesson is counted.

Can I change my booking later on?

Absolutely! You can change your booking anytime. The money paid for the previous booking will be recalculated to the new one.

Will you renew my booking automatically?

Unfortunately, we do not have a feature to support this yet.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer 1 free lesson for 10 lessons you paid.

Can I request a refund?

Unfortunately, we currently do not accept refund. However, you are able to let another person to take over your lesson count.

Application
How do I sign up for tennis lessons?

To enroll in our tennis lessons, you can contact us via our WhatsApp or fill in the registration form to complete the process. Your application will be checked shortly.

What equipment do I need to bring for the tennis lessons?

You can bring your own sport gear and water. If you have your own racket, you can bring it too.

Are there any additional fees or equipment costs beyond the lesson fee?

Yes, there are. The coaching fee includes the lesson itself, but it does not cover court expenses. If you choose to use public courts, there is an additional charge of $10 in addition to the coaching fee. Participants are required to provide their own equipment, including tennis rackets and appropriate attire. Feel free to reach out in advance if you have any further questions about these arrangements.

Is there a minimum age requirement for tennis lessons?

Yes! We offer 1 free lesson for 10 lessons you paid.

General
How do you handle players with different learning styles and preferences in your coaching sessions?

We recognize and accommodate diverse learning styles by tailoring our coaching methods to suit individual preferences.

Is it okay if I do not have a tennis racket for the tennis lesson?

While our tennis coaches can offer a racket for your use, we recommend acquiring your own tennis racket. It's beneficial to have a racket that suits your playing style and preferences. Feel free to seek guidance from our tennis coach on selecting a racket that's suitable for you, as you'll want to become accustomed to your own racket during your lessons!

Is it necessary to have tennis shoes for the tennis lesson?

Absolutely! We advise getting a pair before you begin. Tennis shoes offer superior stability, with reinforced sides that facilitate easy direction changes during play. Using running shoes may cause your feet to shift and hinder proper push-off. However, if you're just testing the waters in tennis for a brief period, your regular sport shoes will suffice!

Do you open on holidays?

Yes! We open during holidays so you can apply for a lesson anytime.
