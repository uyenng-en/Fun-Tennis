Overview: Children have a great capacity to learn and typically pick up new skills quickly. It is therefore advantageous to learn to play tennis at a young age, as they will grow up to have a solid foundation in a sport that can be enjoyed for life.

Our coaches will introduce tennis in a fun and engaging way. We will build your kid’s interest in tennis through meaningful activities that train hand-eye coordination, balance, and agility. Your child will feel a sense of pride from being able to hit his or her first ball over the net!
